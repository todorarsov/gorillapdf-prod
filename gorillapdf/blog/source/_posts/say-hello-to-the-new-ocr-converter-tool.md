Exactly two months after the last update, GorillaPDF brings another powerful tool on the table, which amazes with its efficiency and high degree of accuracy.

Meet the new [online OCR application](https://gorillapdf.com/online-ocr "Online OCR") that easily extract text from images, and present in a PDF file, which you can download and use further.
<!-- more -->

OCR or Optical Character Recognition is technology for converting scanned, printed or handwritten document into a computer-encoded text.

Some examples of using OCR are:

* Turn business card information into a contact list. 
* Making a scanned document searchable.
* Extract text from a scanned ebook.

There are so many real-world applications of using OCR, and we are proud to deliver this useful product in the support of digitalization and people who use digital documents in any way.

The live version recognizes 116 languages and uses advanced techniques for low-quality images, resulting in quality and accurate output.

The OCR Converter is free to use without any additional limitations.
